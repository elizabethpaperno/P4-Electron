Dashboard

Use dashboard to generate a map of NYC Resturants fitting filter critera by checking off boxes and hitting submit. Use the previous and next buttons to naviagte through the results. Click on pins to discover more about the restuarant.

Restaurant Surfer is a data visualization tool that utilizes various resturants dataset to provide users the ability to find their dream restaurant. Users can filter by food type, dietary restrictions,and more. Users can also view restaurants on an embedded map and view their Yelp rating, price data, sanitation grade, delivery and takeout options, and whether the restuarants serve alcohol.
